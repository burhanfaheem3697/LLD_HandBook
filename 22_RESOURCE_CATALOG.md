## Book Mapping Rule

For every lesson, map reading this way:

1. Read the phase's primary book chapter that directly names the concept.
2. If no chapter names it, read the nearest chapter covering the design force.
3. Record exact pages from your edition in the lesson notes.
4. Reimplement one example in Java.
5. Solve at least three exercises or create equivalent exercises when the book lacks them.
